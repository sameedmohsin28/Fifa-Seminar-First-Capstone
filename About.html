FIFA: CELEBRATING THE GLOBAL SPORT

Celebrating The Global Sport is an event aimed to promote diversity and inclusion to every region where the great game of football creates any impact. It also aims to provide equal opprtunities to the remarkable talent all over the world and make this world a level playing field.

Please contact us per email for any further questions about FIFA: Celebrating the Global Sport event.
